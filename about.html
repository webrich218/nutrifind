About NutriFind

Our Mission: Simplifying Nutrition

NutriFind was founded with a single goal: to make detailed, accurate nutrition analysis accessible to everyone, regardless of their cooking skill. We understand that tracking calories, macros, and specific micronutrients in home-cooked meals is challenging. Our mission is to bridge the gap between cooking and dietary planning, ensuring you get transparent information without complicated interfaces or manual searching.

Technology and Data Sources

Our calculator is powered by **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**, which allows you to input ingredients exactly as you would write them in a recipe‚Äîe.g., "1.5 cups flour" or "200g salmon."

  • Data Aggregation: We utilize the **CalorieNinja API** as our primary data source, which aggregates nutritional data from comprehensive databases, including the official USDA FoodData Central. This provides broad and reliable coverage for thousands of ingredients.
  • Unit Handling: The platform automatically handles complex kitchen unit conversions (volume to volume, weight to weight) to deliver accurate totals for the entire recipe.

Privacy and Transparency

NutriFind is committed to user privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). All saved recipes are stored **exclusively in your browser's Local Storage** and never leave your device. For more details on compliance, please visit our Privacy Policy and Health Disclaimer on the main calculator page.
