Banana Brownies

Yields: 8 Servings (1 Slice per Serving)

Ingredients (Total Recipe)

  • 300g ripe banana
  • 1/3 cup Dark Chocolate chips
  • 100g Peanut butter
  • 25g cocoa powder (unsweetened)
  • 2 tbsp Milk

Instructions

  1. Mash the bananas thoroughly.
  2. In a separate bowl, warm the peanut butter slightly in the microwave to make it easier to blend.
  3. Combine mashed banana, peanut butter, cocoa powder, half of the chocolate chips, and milk. Mix until just combined.
  4. Transfer the batter into a small baking pan (e.g., 8x8 in).
  5. Top with the rest of the chocolate chips.
  6. Bake in a pre-heated oven at 180¬∞C (350¬∞F) for approximately 20 minutes.
  7. Chill completely before cutting and serving for the best texture.
